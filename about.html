Wanna know who we are?

We happen to be a bunch of tech enthuasits who are trying to bring about a difference in the ways we live and take care of ourselves. Prerna was not always like this, it had gone through many changes, has seen many ups and downs, and what you are viewing right now is the result of the latest rebranding intiaitve we took last month.

Where it all started?

No, we didn't found our company in a garage. Instead, the founding members thought of the original version of the Prerna during a 48-hour long hackathon in their college days. Since then, they never looked back and continued to make this world a better place to live. Our company was officially registered on 12th August, 2014.
